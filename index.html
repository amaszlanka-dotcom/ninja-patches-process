      Cancellation & Refund — Embroidery

      Classic Embroidery — Not Viable

      When embroidery is not viable due to high detail at scale or gradients in the design.

      Hey there, Thank you for your interest in Ninja Patches Embroidery. After reviewing your order, we have determined that embroidery is not viable for the design you submitted due to high detail at scale or the presence of gradients in your design. We have canceled this item and issued a credit to your account. You are welcome to place an order using a more accommodating product type like Full-Color Printed, or submit a revised embroidery design.
      Cancellation & Refund — Chenille

      Chenille — Not Viable

      When chenille is not viable due to level of detail and scale.

      Hey there, Thank you for your interest in Ninja Patches Chenille. After reviewing your order, we have determined that chenille is not viable for the design you submitted due to the level of detail and scale. We have canceled this item and issued a credit to your account. You are welcome to place an order using a more accommodating product type like Full-Color Printed, or submit a revised chenille design (ideally at a larger scale).
      Cancellation & Refund — Woven

      Woven — Not Viable

      When woven patches are not viable due to high detail or gradients.

      Hey there, Thank you for your interest in Ninja Patches Woven. After reviewing your order, we have determined that woven patches are not viable for the design you submitted due to high detail at scale or the presence of gradients in your design. We have canceled this item and issued a credit to your account. You are welcome to place an order using a more accommodating product type like Full-Color Printed, or submit a revised woven design.
      Cancellation & Refund — Leather

      Leather/Faux Leather — Not Viable

      When leather engraving is not viable due to high detail, scale, or multiple colors.

      Hey there, Thank you for your interest in Ninja Patches Leather. After reviewing your order, we have determined that leather engraving is not viable for the design you submitted due to high detail, scale, or multiple colors in your design. We have canceled this item and issued a credit to your account. You are welcome to place an order using a more accommodating product type like Full-Color Printed, or submit an alternative leather-compatible design.
      Proof — Single Stitch Warning

      Single Stitch Quality Notice

      When a proof comes back with single-stitch text — send to customer before production.

      Due to the scale requested for this patch the text could only be embroidered in a "single-stitch" style. This can often lead to an illegible, poor quality look in the final product.
      Escalation — Customer Insists on Hold

      Cannot Keep Customer On Hold

      When a customer insists on waiting for a supervisor while on the call.

      I'm unable to keep you on the line while waiting for a Supervisor/Manager to become available, but I have escalated your request. I will take down your information and follow up with you as soon as possible.
      Escalation — Customer Refuses Info

      Customer Won't Provide Account Info

      When a customer refuses to provide identifying information but requests a manager.

      I understand you'd like to speak with a manager. I can definitely look into that for you, but I'll need a bit of information so I can pull up your order and get you to the right person.

      3D Puff Transfer — Not Viable

      When a Puff Transfer order cannot be produced because the design is multicolor.

      Hey there, Thank you for your interest in Ninja Transfers 3D Puff Transfers. We have canceled and refunded your Puff Transfer order. Your design is a multicolor image which cannot be produced as a Puff Transfer. 3D Puff transfers can only be produced as single-color prints and work best with minimal detail. We highly suggest reviewing examples on the Puff Transfer page to determine whether your artwork is a good fit before placing a new order.
      Policies & Rules
      Official Ninja Patches operational policies. These are the rules agents must follow.
      Shopify Order Status Reference (W3os)
      Unreviewed
      New order — not yet placed with a vendor. Can be canceled or changed.
      Waiting on Proof
      Assigned to vendor, waiting on proof development. Can be edited, canceled, or changed.
      Proof Approved
      Proof approved for production. Very close to production — still editable but act fast.
      Proof Sent to Customer
      Customer reviewing proof. Ideal time for feedback or cancellation. Still editable.
      Waiting on Revision
      Design revision requested. Changes can be made but will delay further.
      Sent to Production
      CANNOT be canceled or edited. Patches are being made per last approved proof.
      Vendor Shipped
      Finished, in transit to Ninja for quality check. Cannot cancel. Only NDA upgrade allowed (US only).
      No Proof, Sent to Prod
      24 pcs below, no proof — order can proceed directly to production. Still editable/cancelable.
      Customer Action Needed
      Order on hold — customer needs to confirm change, send new design, or update payment.
      Proof Timing & Policies
      Turnaround
      Standard proof turnaround: 2–3 business days
      Free Proofs
      Proofs are free on orders over $300. If customer with order over $300 opts out, remove and notify service channel.
      No Rush
      Proofs cannot be rushed under any circumstances.
      Production Start
      Production begins ONLY after proof is approved.
      Vendor Always Proofs
      The vendor will always send a proof whether or not the customer purchased one.
      25 pcs+
      Orders 25 pcs and above always go to "Waiting on Proof" status regardless of proof purchase.
      NinjaFast Policy
      Definition
      Rush production program for accelerated turnaround. Rush fee calculated at checkout.
      Timeline
      5 business days after proof approval. Typically 6-10 business days total delivery after proof approval.
      No proof countdown
      Starts the day AFTER order is placed.
      With proof countdown
      Starts the day AFTER proof approval.
      NDA Not Allowed
      Shipping upgrades to Next Day Air are NOT allowed on NinjaFast orders.
      Vendor Confirmation
      Must confirm vendor can meet timeline: "This is a Ninja Fast order that needs to go right to production and ship within 5 business days. Can you meet this timeline?"
      Upgrade Approval
      Do NOT offer NinjaFast upgrades without approval from Mercedes, Michel, or Gabriel.
      NDA (Next Day Air) Shipping Policy
      Eligibility
      All 4 must be true: (1) No tracking on order yet, (2) Not NinjaFast or Expedited, (3) Within Contiguous US, (4) Standard turnaround
      Exclusions
      Canada, Alaska, Hawaii — NDA NOT available
      NinjaFast/Expedited
      Cannot adjust shipping — already has the quickest shipping available
      Reason for Edit
      Always enter "Cx upgrade to NDA" as the reason for edit in Shopify
      Escalation Policy
      When to Escalate
      ALL 3 must be true: (1) Customer requests Supervisor/Manager, (2) You have provided all available info, (3) You have consulted Team Leads
      Where
      Post in the #ninja-transfers-service Slack channel
      Include
      Customer account information + clear summary of the issue + reason for escalation
      Do NOT
      Do not keep the customer waiting on the line for a Supervisor/Manager
      Lost in Transit Policy
      Definition
      No tracking update for 3-4 business days = potentially lost in transit
      Resolutions
      Reprint order (same turnaround), Credit the order, or Refund the order
      $500+ Orders
      Must obtain team lead approval BEFORE offering a refund as resolution
      Tax Exempt Policy
      Submission
      Customers upload tax-exempt forms directly on the website
      Review
      Form sent to Accounting/Tax department for review — they email the customer with approval or rejection
      Until Approval
      Customers continue to be charged tax on orders until they receive official approval email
      Vendor Assignment Rules
      First-Time Customer
      Can be assigned to any preferred vendor based on the Vendor Chart for Patch Types
      Repeat Customer
      MUST be assigned to the same vendor who handled the previous order — different vendors have slight differences in color tones, stitching style, border stitching, material texture, and print output
      Reorder Process
      Check customer's previous order → identify original vendor → assign new order to same vendor
      Add Name to Tags
      Immediately add your name in Shopify tags when opening an order to prevent duplicate handling
